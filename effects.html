Trigger Warning: Mentions of self-harm and suicide.

Effects of cyberbullying

Like bullying victims, victims of cyberbullying feel helpless too. The bullying might not harm them physically, but a lot happens in their mind. Cyberbullying can have an impact on the victims mentally and emotionally, it causes low self-esteem, and it will make the victim harm themself.

  • Mentally
    Victims will slowly blame everything on themselves, and lose interest in everything. All they think about is
    the next move of their bully, which results in anxiety.
  • Emotionally
    Victims might feel angry, upset, or embarrassed. They live in constant fear, always thinking about their
    situation. this can easily lead to mood swings, depression etc.
  • Self-esteem
    Cyberbullying can cause low self-esteem for the victims. They will feel bad about how they look, how they
    act, and who they hangout with depending on the type of cyberbullying. They will start doubting and think
    about if they are worthy or valuable to anyone.
  • Self-harm
    Self-harm and suicide usually happen when the victim has been bullied over a long period of time non-stop.
    They will feel like the only way to end their pain, is through ending it all together. They feel hopeless and eventually
    give up.
